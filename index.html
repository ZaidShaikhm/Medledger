Two products, one ecosystem

Pick the track that fits
your operation.

Retail pharmacies and wholesale distributors have fundamentally different billing workflows. MedLedger ships both — under one login.

Counter billing in seconds

Scan, bill, and print a GST invoice in under 30 seconds — works with any USB or Bluetooth barcode scanner.

Batch-wise stock tracking

Every medicine tracked to its batch, manufacturer, and expiry date. FIFO dispensing enforced automatically.

Automatic reorder alerts

Set minimum stock thresholds per SKU — get alerts before runout or auto-raise purchase orders to suppliers.

Prescription record-keeping

Scan and link prescriptions to invoices. Schedule H & H1 drugs flagged and recorded for drug licence compliance.

Bulk invoice generation

Generate hundreds of GST invoices and e-way bills in a single batch run — with auto-calculated freight and taxes.

Party ledger management

Party-wise outstanding balances, aging reports, and credit-limit enforcement — so collections are data-led.

Retailer ordering portal

Give downstream pharmacies a self-serve portal to place orders, track dispatches, and download their own invoices.

Multi-warehouse inventory

Stock levels, dispatches, and returns stay accurate across every depot and warehouse — in real time.
